About Smack Fitness

Smack Fitness is a certified unisex gym located in the heart of Bommanahalli, Bangalore. We offer a wide range of fitness programs — from weight training and bodybuilding to Zumba, Crossfit, and one-on-one personal coaching. Our certified trainers are passionate about helping every member — beginner or advanced — reach their fitness goals in a supportive and energetic environment.
